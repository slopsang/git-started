Superman

He is my favorite character since I was 10. His name is Superman.

Superman is the sole survivor of the planet Krypton. He was born on Earth when the starcraft landed there. Jonathan and Martha Kent found the infant inside the vessel and brought him to their farm in Smallville, Kansas. The Kents named the infant Clark and raised him as their own son.
